2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Username: A unique identifier you choose
  • Email Address: Used for account verification and communication
  • Password: Stored in encrypted (hashed) format for security
  • User ID: System-generated unique identifier
  • Role: Whether you are a client or administrator
  • Account Status: Active/inactive status and account creation/update timestamps

2.2 Personal Information

  • Age: For personalized fitness and nutrition recommendations
  • Gender: For health calculations and goal setting
  • Height: Measured in centimeters
  • Current Weight: For progress tracking
  • Target Weight: For goal setting
  • Weight Unit Preference: Kilograms (kg) or pounds (lbs)

2.3 Health and Fitness Data

  • Body Weight: Daily weight measurements
  • Steps: Daily step count (if synced with health apps)
  • Cardio Minutes: Cardiovascular exercise duration
  • Sleep Hours: Sleep duration tracking
  • Stress Level: Self-reported stress levels (1-10 scale)
  • Workout Data: Workout type, duration, and related information
  • Physique Check-ins: Photos and body measurements for progress tracking
  • Daily Entries: Notes and observations about your health and fitness journey

2.4 Nutrition Data

  • Meal Logs: Foods and meals you consume
  • Food Items: Names, quantities, and units of food items
  • Meal Photos: Images of meals for AI-powered nutrition analysis
  • Nutrition Goals: Daily calorie, protein, carbohydrate, fat, and fiber goals
  • Saved Meals: Frequently consumed meals for quick logging

2.5 Onboarding and Preferences

  • Fitness Goals: Bulking, cutting, maintenance, muscle gain, or weight loss
  • Activity Level: Sedentary, lightly active, moderately active, very active, or extremely active
  • Workout Preferences: Preferred workout types, duration, and weekly workout goals
  • Medical Conditions: Any health conditions you choose to disclose
  • Dietary Restrictions: Food allergies, intolerances, or dietary preferences
  • Supplements: Information about supplements you take
  • Additional Notes: Any other relevant information you provide

2.6 Device and Usage Information

We may automatically collect information such as device type, operating system, app version, usage data, log data, and permissions (camera, microphone, etc.) with your consent.

2.7 Voice and Image Data

When you use voice or image features, we collect recordings and photos for AI-powered analysis and progress tracking.
